## Feature Overview

### What Is This?
A complete redesign of the Add Product form's ingredients section for Grooming Products. Instead of adding ingredients one-by-one with multiple clicks, sellers can now paste or type all ingredients at once in a simple text format.

### Who Benefits?
- **Sellers:** Get ingredients added 3x faster
- **Users:** Better product information (easier to add many ingredients)
- **Support:** Fewer questions about ingredient entry
- **Platform:** More complete product data

### How It Works
1. Seller types/pastes all ingredients
2. Clicks "Parse & Add Ingredients"
3. Sees preview table with validation
4. Submits form
