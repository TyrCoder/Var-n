## üîÑ Data Flow

### Creating a Review (Buyer ‚Üí Seller)
1. Buyer purchases product
2. Buyer leaves review (1-5 stars + comment)
3. Review appears in seller's Reviews section
4. Status: "Pending" (not yet approved)
5. Seller approves/rejects
6. If approved: Shows on product page
7. If rejected: Deleted

### Creating a Promotion (Seller ‚Üí Buyers)
1. Seller fills promotion form
2. Submits for admin approval
3. Admin reviews promotion
4. If approved:
   - Status: "Active" or "Scheduled"
   - Email notifications sent to previous buyers
   - Promo code automatically generated
5. If rejected:
   - Seller notified
   - Promotion not activated
6. Seller can delete/deactivate at any time

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes

### Approval Status Meanings
- **Pending**: Waiting for admin review (not live yet)
- **Active**: Promotion is currently running
- **Scheduled**: Promotion scheduled for future (not active yet)
- **Inactive**: Promotion ended or was deleted

### Email Notifications
- Sent to buyers who previously purchased the product
- Sent when promotion is approved by admin
- Contains: Product name, original price, discount price, discount amount
- Limited to 100 most recent buyers per promotion

### Discount Types
- **Percentage (%)**: E.g., "20%" takes 20% off the price
- **Fixed (‚Ç±)**: E.g., "‚Ç±500" takes ‚Ç±500 off the price
